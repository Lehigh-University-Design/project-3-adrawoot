Projects

After retiring from professional basketball, Kobe Bryant channeled his creative energy into founding Granity Studios, a multimedia content creation company aimed at inspiring young athletes and telling compelling stories through sports. His passion for storytelling led to the creation of several notable projects. "Dear Basketball," an animated short film based on Kobe's poem announcing his retirement, won an Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film, beautifully illustrating his lifelong love for the game. The Wizenard Series, a set of young adult novels created by Kobe and written by author Wesley King, blends fantasy and sports to teach life lessons, following a young basketball team that encounters magical events that challenge and grow them. "Legacy and the Queen" and "Epoca: The Tree of Ecrof" are novels that explore themes of perseverance and destiny in the settings of elite sports academies. Additionally, "Detail," an ESPN series hosted by Kobe, offers in-depth analysis of current basketball games and players, drawing on his extensive knowledge of the game to educate both young players and seasoned fans.
